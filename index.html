> How_This_Works

This habit tracker uses your browser's localStorage to save your habits. This means:

  • Your habits are private and only visible to you
  • Your data persists between visits on the same device and browser
  • Different users will see their own individual habit trackers
  • Clearing browser data will reset your habit tracker

> Data_Management

Back up your habit data to your computer or restore from a previous backup.

Note: Importing data will overwrite your current habits. Resetting will permanently delete all habits.
